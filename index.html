Dimitris Vogiatzopoulos

I am a skilled game developer with a passion for creating immersive, engaging gaming experiences. Through my work, I strive to transport players to exciting new worlds, challenge them with inventive gameplay mechanics, and bring their gaming dreams to life.

MY PROJECTS

ALGORITHMS AND DATA STRUCTURES TRAINING SIMULATOR

A simulator for algorithms and data structures was developed for my thesis. The simulator allows for gamified learning and enables interaction between teachers and students to enhance learning outcomes. The project was carried out in collaboration with CTLL for the ALIEN project, which aims to improve the quality of higher education through active learning and problem-based learning that prepares students for their professional lives by developing industry-relevant skills.

Cminds

During an internship with CTLL, a game called Cminds was developed with the goal of teaching programming basics to young people in an engaging and entertaining manner. The game introduces fundamental programming concepts such as if and while commands, providing a fun and interactive way for young learners to acquire these essential skills.

TEAMSTAR

As an employee of CTLL, work was done on the TEAMSTAR project, which is a STEM game focused on entrepreneurship. The game requires players to make critical decisions to establish and grow their own businesses, such as hiring or firing employees, in order to expand their business ventures.

CLIMATIC

Climactic is a competitive serious game, based on the design principles of edutainment, which encourages the player to learn about climate change in order to survive each round of play.

ZompieLand

This game features a crowd simulation with 200,000 agents, powering a simple tower defense game. Additionally, it was presented to Epic Games through the framework for crowd simulation provided by the University of Utrecht, where the game was developed.

PirateRun

Pirate Run is an exciting game designed specifically for blind and visually-impaired players. In the game, you play as a pirate who is trying to escape from a disaster by running as far as possible. Along the way, you will encounter various obstacles that you must avoid by listening carefully to auditory cues.

PyramidVR

As an employee for Utrecht University In this project, a VR game is being created where there is a static pyramid and the user can move the top of the pyramid using the main button of the right controller and change the volume. If the volume is equal to the initial volume then the pyramid has green color, otherwise, the pyramid has red color.
